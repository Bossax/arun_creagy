## Execution steps (event-scoped loop)
1) Inventory inbox artifacts; confirm which are “sources” vs “draft deliverables.”
2) Seed the timeline spine with known milestones.
3) From the spine, backfill:
   - Trigger(s) (T-*)
   - Deliverable unit(s) (D-*)
   - Claim(s) (C-*)
4) Identify next imminent event (meeting/workshop/report checkpoint) and create the next executable deliverable artifact (a pack, template, or brief) rather than only updating ledgers.

## Definition of done (for bootstrap)
- Hub/plan exist and link to all ledgers.
- At least 2 triggers and 3 deliverables seeded from real sources.
- Risk/Issue log exists and lists the top 2 operational risks (budget eligibility; representation quality).
