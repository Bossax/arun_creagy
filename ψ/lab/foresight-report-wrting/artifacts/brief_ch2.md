## 1) Chapter purpose (1 paragraph)
Chapter 2 must convert the evidence base into an intelligible map of change affecting children and youth in 3 จชต., distinguishing signals, drivers, and uncertainties, and ending with a set of future tensions that legitimately feed Chapter 3 scenario logic.

## 2) Reader assumptions and what must be explained from zero
- Readers may not know foresight terminology.
- Chapter 2 must teach just enough vocabulary to follow the analysis, without reading like a method appendix.
- Explain why signals are treated as early warnings and how system mapping avoids linear explanations.

## 3) Key messages (3–7)
1) Signals show compounding pressures across learning pathways, household economy, welfare access, trust, and digital exposure.
2) Drivers are extracted from signals and organized to support scenario-building.
3) System dynamics create feedback loops that can trap children and youth into constrained trajectories.
4) A small number of high-impact uncertainties shape divergent futures and justify multiple scenarios.
5) Wild cards are stress-tests for strategy, not dramatic storytelling.

- สัญญาณเชิงโครงสร้าง/เชิงพลวัต/สัญญาณอ่อน.
