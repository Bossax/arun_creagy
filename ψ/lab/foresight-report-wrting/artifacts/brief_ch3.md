## 1) Chapter purpose (1 paragraph)
Chapter 3 must translate Chapter 2 uncertainties into a baseline future plus a coherent set of 4 alternative futures, then articulate a desirable future as a navigational target that Chapter 4 can backcast and stress-test against scenarios.

## 2) Reader assumptions and what must be explained from zero
- Readers may confuse scenarios with predictions.
- Explain how to read scenarios and why multiple futures increase preparedness.
- Define persona use: persona is a device to make system implications tangible, not a fictional story detached from evidence.

## 3) Key messages (3–7)
1) Baseline future is the reference line: what plausibly happens under current trajectories.
2) Scenarios reflect divergent futures driven by critical uncertainties, not imagination.
3) Each scenario must show both opportunities and risks for children and youth.
4) Descriptors enable comparison; do not change the comparison grid mid-chapter.
5) Desirable future is value-anchored and operational: it defines minimum conditions and the direction of travel.

## 4) Required definitions (terms to introduce + preferred Thai wording)
- ภาพอนาคตฐาน, ภาพอนาคตทางเลือก, ฉากทัศน์.
- แกนตรรกะฉากทัศน์, ตัวบ่งชี้ฉากทัศน์.
- บุคลักษณ์ (persona) and day-in-the-life as evidence-anchored synthesis.
- ภาพอนาคตที่พึงประสงค์.

## 5) Evidence requirements

Claims that must be sourced
- Any numeric projections or specific quantitative scenario claims.
- Any named policy or programme that is asserted as widespread or effective.

Permissible qualitative synthesis
- Scenario logics grounded in Chapter 2 drivers and tensions.
- Desirable future grounded in stated values and practical minimum conditions.

## 6) Tables/figures required (and where they come from)
- Scenario axis figure (2x2) and a fixed descriptor table.
- Scenario comparison table at the end of the 4 scenarios.

## 7) Cross-chapter dependencies

Inputs needed from other chapters
- Chapter 2: future tensions, critical uncertainties, and system bottlenecks.

Outputs this chapter promises to later chapters
- A stable scenario set and descriptor matrix for Chapter 4 robustness testing.
- Desirable future vision and minimum conditions.
