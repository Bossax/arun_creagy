## How to use

- Fill this worksheet **before** drafting grounded prose.
- For each subsection, list what sources exist and whether they meet sufficiency.
- If a section is “No-Go”, the draft must keep placeholders and avoid factual claims.

Legend:
- **Go** = sufficient sources to write grounded prose with citations
- **Caution** = can draft partially; keep placeholders for missing sub-claims
- **No-Go** = structure-only; no factual claims
