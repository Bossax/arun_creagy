# Task 5.5 Scope — Knowledge Sets on Climate Risks & Impacts

## Purpose
Define a TOR‑compliant knowledge‑set scope that feeds Phase‑1 NCAIF content without over‑claiming platform build.

## TOR anchors (CRDB - TOR 5.5)
- Review studies from the past 10 years across **6 NAP sectors**.
- Select **at least 10 significant studies** and synthesize them.
- Produce dissemination media: **≥20 infographics**, **≥10 academic articles**, **≥12 roll‑up exhibition sets**.

## NCAIF alignment (Phase‑1 content layer)
The knowledge sets are the **primary explanatory layer** for NCAIF pages:
- climate foundation and hazard explainers
- sector risk and adaptation narratives
- interpretation of risk maps and limitations
- decision‑maker briefing content

## Deliverable architecture

### Layer A — Evidence review matrix
- Sector
- Hazard
- Geography
- Time horizon
- Study type
- Key findings
- Policy relevance
- NCAIF page relevance

### Layer B — Synthesis set (minimum 10 articles)
Recommended article set aligned to NCAIF sitemap:
1. NCAIF overview + why it exists
2. Climate trends and projections in Thailand
3. Heat risk
4. Heavy rain/flood risk
5. Drought risk
6. Water management risk + adaptation priorities
7. Agriculture & food security risk + adaptation priorities
8. Tourism risk + adaptation priorities
9. Public health risk + adaptation priorities
10. Natural resources + human settlement/security synthesis for place‑based decisions

### Layer C — Media package
- **Infographics (≥20):** mapped to articles + user journeys
- **Roll‑up sets (≥12):** grouped by climate foundation, sector risks, and adaptation responses
