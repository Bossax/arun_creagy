## Drafting implication for Chapter 2

The strongest factual thread for subsection **2.2** is now clear:

1. DCCE already has a large but fragmented web/content estate.
2. The spatial risk-map product is the most mature current climate-risk product, but it is bounded in scale and interpretation.
3. Multiple partner agencies already maintain their own systems, portals, dashboards, registries, and baseline-data hubs.
4. The landscape therefore contains real assets, but not yet a unified user-facing national information structure.
