| SCP and other program-specific portals | Host program-specific content and tools (e.g. sustainable consumption and production, sectoral initiatives) that include adaptation-relevant information | SCP portals and other program sub-portals linked from the DCCE main website | Program-specific pages, case studies, guidance materials, and tools; some may include adaptation-relevant examples and measures | Use program-level datasets and narrative content; detailed data provenance and governance are not described in current notes | Portals are siloed by program, with limited cross-linking into a national adaptation information narrative; adaptation-relevant content is scattered across pages and not indexed through an adaptation-cycle or area-profile lens |
