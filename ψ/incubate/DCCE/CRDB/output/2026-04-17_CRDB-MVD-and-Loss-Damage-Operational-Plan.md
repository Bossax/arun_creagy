## Phase 1 operational scope

1. Define minimum viable dataset fields for Loss & Damage operational use.
2. Define observation envelope conventions (metadata + payload expectations).
3. Define intake and revision control behavior:
   - ingestion checkpoint,
   - QC/validation flags,
   - update/revision handling.
4. Assign rail posture and publication constraints by field or product class.

## Required outputs

- MVD field and schema specification draft
- Intake template draft and mapping logic notes
- QC/revision flag vocabulary aligned with governance track
- Crosswalk to CDM subject areas and NCAIF service expressions

## Validation checkpoints

1. IT discussion: data structure feasibility and implementation boundary.
2. Workshop: provider/processor reality on required fields and update cadence.
3. FGD3: internal ownership and sustainable operating model.
