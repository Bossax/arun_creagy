### 2. Rice adaptation in Lower Chao Phraya Basin under CMIP6/SSP

**Title:** Exploring the impacts of climate change and identifying potential adaptation strategies for sustainable rice production in Thailand’s Lower Chao Phraya Basin through crop simulation modeling  
**Year:** 2024  
**Sector:** Agriculture / Water  
**Hazard Focus:** Heat stress, Drought, Water scarcity  
**Geography within Thailand:** Lower Chao Phraya River Basin (includes BMR upstream context)  
**Study Type:** Process‑based crop simulation (DSSAT‑CERES‑Rice) with NEX‑GDDP‑CMIP6 (SSP1‑2.6, SSP2‑4.5, SSP5‑8.5)
