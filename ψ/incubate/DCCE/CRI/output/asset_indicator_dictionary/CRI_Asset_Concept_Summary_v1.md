# CRI Asset Concept Summary v1 (Phase 3)

## Methodological framing
- Asset concepts are interpreted as **enabling stock (structural potential)**, not realized resilience outcomes.
- Governance/process capacity remains the activation logic required to convert stock into service performance.
- Concept use is constrained by safe-interpretation guardrails (wealth-bias control, accessibility/distribution checks, threshold/lock-in caution).

  - Macro-financial translation sensitivity under financial/economic buffer stock.
  - Amenity-to-climate service ambiguity under built-form/public-realm stock.
- Governance-process drift row `AIR-011` is excluded from canonical asset rows and retained only as governance activation reference.
