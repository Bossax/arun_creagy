# CRI Asset Indicator Concepts: P3 (Spatial & Ecological Transformation)

| asset_concept | description | capacity_type | SETS | evidence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Mangroves and coastal marshes | Serves as a long-term adaptive nature-based defense that provides coastal protection services by attenuating wave energy, reducing storm surges, and dynamically adapting to sea-level rise through lateral inland movement. | Adaptive | Ecological | "IPCC_AR6_WGII_CCP2-Cities and Settlements.pdf" |
| Bioswales, rain gardens, bioretention ponds, and constructed wetlands | Hybrid engineered-ecological assets that enable "Safe-to-Fail" resilience by capturing, retaining, and absorbing stormwater runoff to prevent the failure of centralized gray drainage infrastructure during heavy precipitation. | Adaptive | Bundle (Ecological and Technological) | "A social-ecological-technological systems framework for urban ecosystem services.pdf" |
| Flood retention zones and "Room for the River" areas (including vegetated flood mitigation buffers) | Designated spatial planning areas (such as agricultural lands or buffer zones) intended to safely overflow and absorb excess water when river or drainage capacities are exceeded. This shifts the system from "Fail-Safe" rigid structures to a "Safe-to-Fail" framework that protects populated urban centers. | Transformative | Bundle (Social, Ecological, and Technological) | "Leveraging SETS resilience capabilities for safe-to-fail infrastructure under climate change.pdf", "A framework for urban climate resilience.pdf" |
| Land-use zoning (restricting floodplain development) | An institutional and spatial planning asset that physically distances populations and property from hazard-prone areas (like floodplains), facilitating long-term transformation by substituting the need for hardened physical defenses with social/regulatory avoidance. | Transformative | Social | "Leveraging SETS resilience capabilities for safe-to-fail infrastructure under climate change.pdf" |
| Cold air parcels and ventilation corridors | Spatial environmental assets in the urban form that regulate air quality and facilitate long-term thermal comfort and ventilation to mitigate the urban heat island effect. | Adaptive | Ecological | "Indicators for Monitoring Urban Climate Change Resilience and Adaptation.pdf" |
