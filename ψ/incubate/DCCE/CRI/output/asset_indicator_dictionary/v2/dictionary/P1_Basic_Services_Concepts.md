| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| Diversified geographic food supply sources | Ensures food supply is maintained by sourcing from varied geographic areas, providing flexibility if one region is affected by climate stress. | Adaptive | Bundle (Social, Ecological, Technological) | "A framework for urban climate resilience.pdf" |
| Food and medicine buffer stocks | Maintains a high stock/flow balance to provide redundancy in case of an unexpected supply disruption. | Coping | Technological (Logistics/Storage) | "A framework for urban climate resilience.pdf" |
| Backup generators | Offers redundant spare capacity for crucial services to accommodate unexpected power losses during extreme climate events. | Coping | Technological | "A framework for urban climate resilience.pdf" |
| Multiple transportation modes, capacities, and access routes | Provides flexibility, redundancy, and multiple pathways for transporting key goods and people if primary routes or modes are disrupted. | Adaptive | Technological | "A framework for urban climate resilience.pdf" |
