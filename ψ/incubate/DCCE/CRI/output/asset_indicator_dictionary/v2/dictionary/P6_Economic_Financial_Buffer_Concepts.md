| asset_concept | description | capacity_type | SETS | evidence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Financial instruments (contingency funds, operating funds, capital funds, etc.) | Provides the necessary capital and financial buffers to ensure the economic security of the community, allowing the municipality and local businesses to manage interruptions, prepare for, and quickly respond to disasters. | Coping | Social (Social-Economic domain) | "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
| Insurance (domestic and non-domestic) and social welfare | Acts as a critical business mitigation and recovery strategy that guarantees economic security by compensating for financial losses and buffering the community against the immediate shocks of disruptions. | Coping | Social (Social-Economic domain) | "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
| Diversity of business / Diverse economic structure and livelihood strategies | Makes the urban economy more robust, flexible, and redundant when facing the uncertainty of climate impacts. A diverse economy avoids the extreme vulnerability associated with reliance on a single industry, helping the community retain businesses and resist the negative impacts of economic decline. | Adaptive | Social (Social-Economic domain) | "Indicators for Monitoring Urban Climate Change Resilience and Adaptation.pdf", "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
