# CRI Asset Indicator Concepts: P2 (Security & Disaster Management)

| asset_concept | description | capacity_type | SETS | evidence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Breakwaters, sea walls, dikes, and barriers (including "super-levees") | Hard engineering protective structures designed to resist extreme weather, reduce coastal flooding, and contain storm surges | Coping | Technological | "IPCC_AR6_WGII_CCP2-Cities and Settlements.pdf" |
| Oversized/backup storm drainage pipelines, spillways, and pumps | Hardened infrastructure components that drain or store excess water from intense precipitation to prevent structural failures and mitigate urban flooding | Coping | Technological | "Leveraging SETS resilience capabilities for safe-to-fail infrastructure under climate change.pdf" |
| Flood monitoring and early warning systems | Technologies and plans that detect impending hazard signals (like floods) and communicate alerts, enabling populations and governments to rapidly organize, evacuate, and respond | Adaptive | Bundle (Technological and Social) | "A framework for urban climate resilience.pdf", "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
| Flood shelters / Shelter and relief facilities | Facilities that provide immediate physical refuge, safety, and relief to displaced populations during and after extreme hazard events | Coping | Technological | "A framework for urban climate resilience.pdf", "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
| Emergency communication infrastructure | Specialized networks that ensure critical information flows and communication remain functional before, during, and after a disaster | Adaptive | Technological | "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
| Emergency operation centers | Centralized facilities that integrate different agencies and organizations to coordinate real-time disaster response, emergency planning, and resource deployment | Adaptive | Bundle (Social and Technological) | "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
| Stockpiles of supplies | Individual and community savings of essential items to provide redundancy and relief in the event of an unexpected disruption | Coping | Technological | "Urban Resilience Assessment - Multiple Dimentions, Criteria, and Indicators.pdf" |
