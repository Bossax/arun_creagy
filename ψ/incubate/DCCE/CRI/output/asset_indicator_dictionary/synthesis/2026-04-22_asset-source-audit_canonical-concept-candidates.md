## 2) Major duplicate / near-duplicate / competing labels needing consolidation logic

1. **Green-system duplicates**
   - Competing labels: “urban vegetation/street trees” (`AIR-001`) vs “urban green infrastructure” (`AIR-017`) vs “urban green space/community forestry” (`AIR-043`) plus inventory-program exemplars (`AIR-014`, `AIR-018`).
   - Consolidation logic: one canonical concept for **urban green ecological stock**, with programmatic/digital variants treated as sub-types or indicator candidates.

2. **Flood-water infrastructure overlap**
   - Competing labels: bioswales/retention (`AIR-004`) vs storage/drainage capacity (`AIR-022`) vs adapted sewer (`AIR-036`) vs flood-compatible road (`AIR-007`).
   - Consolidation logic: one canonical **stormwater and flood-routing asset system** concept with sub-components by function (capture, conveyance, overflow routing, treatment).

3. **Hard coastal protection hierarchy overlap**
   - Competing labels: breakwaters/sea walls/dikes (`AIR-020`) and giant seawalls (`AIR-026`).
   - Consolidation logic: canonical **hard coastal defense stock** concept, preserving “mega-project” variant as high lock-in/high safeguard-risk subtype.

4. **Resilience engineering language overlap**
   - Competing labels: fail-safe infrastructure (`AIR-008`), technological fixes (`AIR-015`), robust structures (`AIR-040`), redundancy (`AIR-041`), decentralization/flexibility (`AIR-042`).
   - Consolidation logic: canonical **infrastructure resilience architecture** concept family with explicit anti-lock-in qualifiers.

5. **Human capability overlap**
   - Competing labels: human capital (`AIR-046`) vs diverse skills/collective knowledge (`AIR-051`) vs workforce qualifications/literacy (`AIR-055`).
   - Consolidation logic: canonical **human capability stock** with scale-specific indicators (population-wide vs community operational skill pools).

6. **Financial buffer overlap**
   - Competing labels: NFA (`AIR-047`), public support instruments (`AIR-049`), savings (`AIR-053`), insurance/contingency (`AIR-054`), microfinance (`AIR-057`).
   - Consolidation logic: canonical **financial resilience buffers** concept with level tags (macro/public/household/community).

7. **Potential category-drift rows**
   - “infrastructure regulations and institutions” (`AIR-011`) behaves as governance process, not stock.
   - Consolidation logic: classify as governance-activation link reference, not canonical asset concept row.

## 4) Placeholder / weak-evidence / exclude-from-v1 flags

- Experimental coastal forms (`AIR-024`, part of `M-013`): keep as placeholder due to stated experimental stage and context sensitivity.
