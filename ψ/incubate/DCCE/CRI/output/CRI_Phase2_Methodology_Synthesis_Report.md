# CRI Phase 2 Methodology Synthesis Report: The Asset-Governance Bridge

## 1. Objective and Trigger (E-CRI-030)
Based on stakeholder insights captured on 2026-04-22, the CRI project pivoted to include **Asset-Based (Stock) Indicators** alongside the established **Governance (Process) Pillars**. This transition serves as a conceptual bridge, moving stakeholders from a static "Hardware" view of resilience toward a dynamic "Multiplicative" view.

## 2. The Synthesis Process
The derivation of the final Phase 3/4 artifacts followed a disciplined three-stage forensic pipeline:

### Stage A: Literature-Led Extraction (The SETS Layer)
- **Sources**: Deep scan of Social-Ecological-Technological Systems (SETS) scholarship (McPhearson et al., 2022; Kim et al., 2022).
- **Output**: Extraction of 57 raw "Asset-Indicator Rows" (AIR-001 to AIR-057) representing physical, ecological, and social stocks.

### Stage B: Concept Hardening (The Hardware Layer)
- **Transformation**: Flattening and clustering of the 57 raw rows into **11 Hardened Concepts (HC-01 to HC-11)**.
- **Category Purity**: Explicit removal of "governance-drift" rows (e.g., AIR-011) to ensure the Asset layer represents only **Resilience Potential (Hardware)**.

### Stage C: Linkage Analysis (The Software Bridge)
- **Mapping**: Each Hardened Concept was cross-walked against the **6 Institutional Readiness Pillars** (v3).
- **Logic**: Resilience is modeled as $S = A \times G$ (Service = Asset Stock multiplied by Governance Activation).
- **M1 Identification**: Surfacing of **Functional Mismatches** where the v3 "Software" lacks the specific "Drivers" required to activate specialized SETS Hardware.

- `CRI_Asset_Concept_Summary_v1.md`: The 11 canonical stocks.
- `CRI_Asset_Tagging_Dictionary_v1.md`: The operational worksheet with "Thai Anchors."
