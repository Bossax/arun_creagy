# WMO-NFCS Claim Register

## Purpose
Track **reusable project claims**: intermediate statements that connect evidence to decisions and deliverables.

This is not “truth.” It is the project’s explicit reasoning surface, kept traceable to sources.

## Usage
- Append claims (C-*) as they become operational.
- Each claim must link to at least one source artifact.
- Use **Confidence** to keep uncertainty explicit.
