### **Architectural Impact Analysis**

The mapping reveals a **Data Integration Mandate (Art. 163)**. This is a critical discovery for our data architecture. It provides the legal "teeth" to enforce **schema standardization** across agencies.

Without this Markdown record, the requirement for an **Integrated Data Hub** might be viewed as a technical choice; here, it is identified as a statutory obligation.
