## **3.2 Implementation Roadmap (Priority Activities)**

Below is a refined extract of high-priority activities identified for the 2025–2026 implementation cycle.

### **Pillar 1: Observations & Monitoring (O&M)**

- **Activity 1.1.1:** Conduct a gap assessment of observation networks in climate-vulnerable and rapidly urbanizing areas.
    
    - _Output:_ Gap analysis report and data-gap maps.
        
    - _Timeframe:_ Short-term (S).
        
- **Activity 1.2.3:** Establish a shared metadata catalogue and station registry for climate-related observation systems.
    
    - _Technical Goal:_ Centralize metadata for interoperability across DCCE and TMD systems.
        

### **Pillar 2: Climate Services Information System (CSIS)**

- **Activity 2.1.1:** Design and establish an integrated climate data and information architecture linking national and sectoral datasets.
    
    - _Output:_ National integrated architecture design.
        
    - _Timeframe:_ Medium-term (M).
        
- **Activity 2.4.1:** Develop impact-based forecasting (IbF) models for heat, flood, landslide, storm, and drought.
    
    - _Output:_ Operational modules for urban-specific risks.
        

### **Pillar 4: User Interface Platform (UIP)**

- **Activity 4.1.1:** Establish a National User Interface Platform (UIP) for the Human Settlement and Security sector.
    
    - _Output:_ Sector-specific portal linking providers and city-level planners.
        
    - _Governance:_ Collaborative management between DCCE and DDPM.

## **3.4 Risk & Data Governance Considerations**

To ensure the sustainability of the **DCCE data ecosystem**, the architecture must address the following risks identified in the `Print out strategy_Thailand` sheet:

1. **Data Fragmentation (Risk Level: High):** The "Response Entities" for Goal 1 are spread across multiple ministries. Without the **Metadata Catalogue (Activity 1.2.3)**, the system will face semantic confusion.
    
2. **Budget Continuity (Concern):** Many activities list "Source of Fund" as yet-to-be-determined. I recommend aligning these with the **Climate Change Act's** mandated National Information Center funding.
    
3. **GESI Integration:** Goal 5 specifically mandates **Activity 5.4.2**—developing GESI standards (Gender, Equality, and Social Inclusion) for inclusive warning communication, ensuring no "Last Mile" resident is left behind.
