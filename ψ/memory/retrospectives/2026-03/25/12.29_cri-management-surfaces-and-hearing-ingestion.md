## Lessons Learned

- CRDB-style project patterns (Hub + plan + evidence registry + coverage map) scale well to other projects like CRI, but only if legacy management surfaces are explicitly retired and marked as archived with `superseded_by` pointers.
- Public hearings and consultations become truly reusable only when they are ingested as a small set of well-typed artifacts: cleaned transcripts, a concise summary note, and a few curated interpretation notes (decisions/signals, methodology implications, governance signals) wired into the evidence registry.
- It is better to treat AI-output indices as transitional scaffolding: once an evidence registry exists, that registry plus targeted synthesis notes should be the primary map of sources, and the old index can be preserved as historical evidence rather than a live control surface.
