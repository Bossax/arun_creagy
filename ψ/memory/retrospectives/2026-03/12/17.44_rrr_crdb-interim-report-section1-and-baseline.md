## Lessons Learned
- For interim reporting, a progress-first narrative is stronger than an artifact-first narrative when the sponsor's understanding has evolved during the work.
- Section 1 becomes coherent when the methodology chain is explicit: interviews -> conceptual modeling rationale -> standards alignment -> predecessor review -> current-state diagnosis -> Phase 1 architecture stance.
- CDM and MVPs should be written as explanatory devices tied to evidence and constraints, not presented as isolated outputs.
