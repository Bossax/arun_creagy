## Lessons Learned
- **The "Mirror" Effect**: Presenting a "Map" of what is already known is the fastest way to provoke experts into giving you high-signal corrections.
- **Role-Product Matchmaking**: Stakeholders are most productive when they are co-creating a specific tool that will help their own daily job.
- **Asymmetry as the Enemy**: The primary value of a multi-stakeholder workshop isn't just the "data list"; it's ensuring everyone in the room has the same mental map of the landscape.
