## Lessons Learned

1. **Dictionary review benefits from a separate synthesis artifact before direct edits.** Creating a dedicated review summary and concept table made the subsequent editing plan much clearer than trying to modify the canonical dictionary directly while reading comments.

3. **Handoff + plan files provide a clean bridge between research and implementation.** Capturing this session’s conclusions in both a handoff note and a next-session plan stabilises the work so that dictionary v3 thinking can happen later without losing today’s reasoning.
