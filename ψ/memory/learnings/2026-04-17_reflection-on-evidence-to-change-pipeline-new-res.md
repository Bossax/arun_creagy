# Reflection on Evidence-to-Change Pipeline: New research is registered as Evidenc

Reflection on Evidence-to-Change Pipeline: New research is registered as Evidence (E-ID), gaps/insights spark a Trigger (T-ID), and structural shifts are formalized in the Change Log (CH-ID). This ensures project evolution is traceable, grounded, and 'nothing is deleted.' Used specifically to pivot CRDB from a monolithic website view to a decoupled 'Content-as-Data' ecosystem.
