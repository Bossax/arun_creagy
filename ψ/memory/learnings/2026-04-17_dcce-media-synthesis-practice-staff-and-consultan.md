# DCCE Media Synthesis Practice: Staff and consultants frequently produce communic

DCCE Media Synthesis Practice: Staff and consultants frequently produce communication media (e.g., for Facebook) using external research. The final outputs are published, but the underlying structured data is often lost ('orphan knowledge'). This necessitates an EDM update introducing a 'Source Registration Protocol' that models `COMMUNICATION_ASSET`, `EXTERNAL_REFERENCE`, and `EVIDENCE_PACKAGE` to govern data provenance without requiring full hosting.
