# Research workflow alignment (Sensing → Forge → Harvest → Rhythm)

## Your workflow preference (operationalized)

### 1) Sensing (asynchronous capture)
**Goal**: don’t interrupt deep work to organize.

**File-system support**:
- Raw/temporary items land in inbox queues (triage later):
  - [`ψ/inbox/_triage_from_active/`](ψ/inbox/_triage_from_active/:1)
  - [`ψ/inbox/_triage_from_writing_notes/`](ψ/inbox/_triage_from_writing_notes/:1)

### 2) Forge (project deliverables)
**Goal**: keep project boundaries + drafting context tight.

**File-system support**:
- Active deliverables and plans live in incubate hubs:
  - [`ψ/incubate/DCCE/CRDB/Hub.md`](ψ/incubate/DCCE/CRDB/Hub.md:1)
  - [`ψ/incubate/DCCE/CRDB/plan.md`](ψ/incubate/DCCE/CRDB/plan.md:1)

### 3) Harvest (atomization)
**Goal**: extract durable insights from client-specific work.

**File-system support**:
- Write atomic notes into [`ψ/memory/learnings/`](ψ/memory/learnings/:1)

### 4) Rhythm (meta reflection)
**Goal**: sessions end with a traceable recap/retro + next steps.

**File-system support**:
- Retrospectives: [`ψ/memory/retrospectives/`](ψ/memory/retrospectives/:1)
