# When designing national coordination workshops for climate services, avoid askin

When designing national coordination workshops for climate services, avoid asking for 'institutional buy-in' from participants who lack the authority to bind their agencies. Instead, frame the session as a 'Technical Feasibility Audit' where participants act as Subject Matter Experts finding implementation hurdles. This extracts a high-value Gap Analysis and prioritized roadmap for the development team while maintaining participant energy and psychological safety. Use neutral, professional terminology instead of consultant jargon.
