# Shift urban resilience asset frameworks from form-based taxonomy (e.g. 'seawalls

Shift urban resilience asset frameworks from form-based taxonomy (e.g. 'seawalls') to mission-based functional pillars (e.g. 'Coping Hardware'). This eliminates the 'Geographic Adoption Wall' and ensures methodology remains stable across diverse geographies while maintaining symmetry with governance pillars (M = A x G). Functional roles are more stable and scalable than physical forms.
