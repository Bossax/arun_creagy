# Learning — DCCE org restructure should be reflected in CDM + governance design

**Date**: 2026-03-27 (Asia/Bangkok)

## Pattern

DCCE has a planned internal reorganization (briefing: Mar 20, 2026). Director Toey noted that CRDB’s **Conceptual Data Model (CDM)** and **data governance** should explicitly align to this new structure.

Treat the org structure as an instrument for governance design:

- Assign **data owners / stewards** per domain/entity based on the reorganized divisions/groups.
- Make approval paths clear (e.g., department-level oversight groups like Legal/Ethics reporting directly to the department).
- Distinguish and allocate roles for **data governance** vs **model governance** across the new structure.
- Ensure technical ownership and integration responsibilities are consistent with the placement of **Digital Technology & Central Database** and the **Research Center**.
