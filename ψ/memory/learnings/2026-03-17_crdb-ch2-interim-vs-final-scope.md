For multi-stage reports, separate **final report structure** from the **interim report slice**: preserve the full Chapter 2 framing for the eventual final report, but scope the interim Chapter 2 tightly to (1) reviewing the current data product landscape, (2) synthesizing interview evidence on what data stakeholders generate and need, and (3) summarizing use cases for climate risk and impact information. Treat the writing plan for the interim as a constrained subset of the long-term outline, not a competing structure.
