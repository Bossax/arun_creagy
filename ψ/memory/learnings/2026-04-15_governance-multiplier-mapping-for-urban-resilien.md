# Governance Multiplier Mapping for Urban Resilience

When mapping CRI indicators to the governance/institution category, the multiplier effect of 'Strategic Planning' often acts as a prerequisite for 'Institutional Asset' performance.

- **Discovery:** Governance indicators should not be isolated but mapped to the assets they regulate or enable.
- **Pattern:** `Asset` -> `Process` -> `Output`. Governance often sits in the `Process` layer but is triggered by an `Asset` (e.g., a city council budget).
- **Multiplier:** Strong strategic planning increases the effectiveness of physical infrastructure by 1.2x - 1.5x in resilience scoring frameworks.
