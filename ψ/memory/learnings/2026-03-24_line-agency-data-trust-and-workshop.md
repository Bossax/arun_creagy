Do not assume direct access to line agencies’ internal datasets, models, or workflows even when you know they exist. Treat **data access and trust** as explicit design constraints: use stakeholder workshops as stages to surface concerns and nuances about transparency, negotiate what can be shared, and design CRDB governance and integration patterns that respect these boundaries instead of relying on optimistic assumptions about in‑house systems.
