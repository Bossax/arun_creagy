# Learning — Align CRDB with DCCE “Digital Technology” workstream even when scope is unclear

**Date**: 2026-03-27 (Asia/Bangkok)

## Pattern

When a sponsor-side leader (Director Toey) flags a parallel DCCE digital/data-system effort (here: **กลุ่มพัฒนาเทคโนโลยีดิจิทัล** hiring a consultant to upgrade DCCE data systems), treat it as both:

- an **integration opportunity** (CRDB’s NCAIF/CDM/governance artifacts can become requirements inputs), and
- a **dependency / scope risk** (objectives and scopes may be unclear; work may proceed without CRDB alignment).

Operationally:

1) Clarify **scope, deliverables, timeline, and decision rights** of the consultant engagement.
2) Position CRDB outputs (NCAIF, CDM, governance gates; model governance) as **semantic + governance backbone** inputs.
3) Avoid duplicating “platform design” work; instead, negotiate interfaces and ownership early.
