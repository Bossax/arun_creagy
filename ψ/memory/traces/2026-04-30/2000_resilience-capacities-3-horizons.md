## Summary of Previous Discussion
The \"3 Capacities\" framework is the backbone of the CRI resilience logic:
1. **Absorptive (Coping)**: Immediate response to shocks (Short-term).
2. **Adaptive**: Learning and incremental adjustment (Medium-term).
3. **Transformative**: Structural redesign and cross-sector integration (Long-term).
