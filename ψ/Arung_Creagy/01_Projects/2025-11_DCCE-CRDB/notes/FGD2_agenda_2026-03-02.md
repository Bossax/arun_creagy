# FGD2 Agenda — Internal DCCE Progress Demo + Feedback (2026-03-02)

## Purpose
- Demonstrate progress on the **Conceptual Data Model (CDM)** and **NCAIF** through tangible activities.
- Bridge **climate-literacy gaps** by using the CDM as a "teaching vehicle" for internal staff.
- Collect **operational feedback** from both data producers (Risk) and internal consumers (M&E/Communication).

## Agenda (150 minutes / 2h30)

1. **Opening + Continuity Bridge** (20 mins)
   -  Summary of FGD1 (Jan 22) findings regarding system fragmentation 
   - Strategic goal: "Govern what you need, just-in-time".

2. **A1: Putting-data-in-a-box (Mentimeter + CDM Demo)** (30 mins)
   -  **Literacy Check**: Classify datasets into Hazard, Exposure, Vulnerability, and Risk 
   - Focus on resolving confusion between raw environmental data and processed risk indicators.

1. **A2: Internal Use-Case Walkthrough** (40 mins) (to be added further by March 6)
   - **M&E Integration**: How the Risk Baseline feeds M&E indicators.
   - **Communication Tiers**: Validating the "light user-journey layer" for public-facing groups.

4. **A3: Governance Phasing (Quality Gates)** (30 mins)
   - Defining "Bronze to Silver" promotion rules for the Risk Assessment Database.
   - Feedback on the feasibility of mandated "Limitations Statements" for all published products.

5. **Feedback Synthesis + Close** (30 mins)

## Agenda (150 minutes / 2h30)

1. **Opening + continuity bridge** (15–20 mins)
   - Why FGD2 now, and how this builds on FGD1.
   - Re-state that this is a **progress demo + feedback** session.

2. **A1: Putting-data-in-a-box (Mentimeter + CDM explanation)** (20–25 mins)
   - CDM explanation (why we need it).
   - Mentimeter quiz: classify datasets into CDM domains.
   - Include commonly confused datasets (flood risk vs flood hazard vs historical hazard).
   - Capture entity priority signals from the group.

3. **A2: Internal use-case walkthrough** (20–25 mins)
   - Use case 1: **Publishing workflow** (draft → review → publish) and QA/QC points.
   - Use case 2: **Climate risk communication tiers** (policy / practitioner / public).
   - Use case 3: **Official dataset search** (authoritative sources + metadata).
   - Ask: Which use case is most urgent? What is missing or unclear?

4. **A3: Governance phasing plan (data quality framing)** (10–15 mins)
   - Present governance phasing as **data quality enablement**.
   - Ask for reactions: missing quality gates? feasibility concerns?

5. **Feedback capture + close** (15–20 mins)
   - Summarize key feedback points.
   - Confirm next steps and how feedback will be incorporated.

## Feedback capture prompts (live)
- Which internal use case should be prioritized first?
- Where do current workflows break (data quality, metadata, approvals)?
- Which dataset types create the most confusion or rework?
- What is missing from the current governance phasing plan?

## Outputs
- Feedback summary (use-case priority + gaps + quality concerns).
- Clear next steps for updating artifacts post-session.

## Notes

- This session does **not** finalize decisions; it prepares for later decision-making.
