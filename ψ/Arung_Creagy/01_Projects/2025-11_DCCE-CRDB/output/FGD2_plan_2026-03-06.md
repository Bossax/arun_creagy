## Objective

1) Demonstrate progress on **CDM + NCAIF** through tangible activities.
2) Collect feedback on **internal operational use cases** and **governance phasing**.
3) Capture a small set of **decisions + named owners** required to ship Phase 1 safely.

Note: this session can be framed as “progress demo + feedback” or “light decision capture”; decide which framing fits the audience.

---

## Facilitation constraints (explicit)

- Assume mixed technical literacy; use concrete workflows rather than architecture jargon.
- Use “You said → We responded” to build continuity with FGD1.
- For each decision prompt, capture:
  - 1 sentence decision
  - owner (role/title)
  - next-step artifact to update

## Agenda (150 minutes / 2h30)

### 1) Opening + continuity bridge (15–20 mins)

**Key message**: “FGD2 is how we turn FGD1 pain points into Phase 1 products that are safe and usable.”

- Remind: this builds on FGD1.
- Show: 1 slide “You said → We responded”.

### 2) A1 — Putting-data-in-a-box (Mentimeter + CDM demo) (20–30 mins)

**Key message**: “CDM is the shared blueprint for what we must record consistently.”

Activity:

- Mentimeter: classify datasets into Hazard / Exposure / Vulnerability / Risk.
- Include commonly confused examples (hazard vs risk, historic vs projected, etc.).

Outputs:

- Prioritization signals for which domains must be “correct first.”

### 3) A2 — Internal use-case walkthrough (20–30 mins)

**Key message**: “We are designing from workflows, not from IT preferences.”

Use-case walkthrough set (pick 2–3):

1) Publishing workflow (draft → review → publish) + minimum QA/QC gate.
2) 3-depth information tiers (policy / practitioners / public).
3) “Which dataset should I use?” baseline endorsement (recommended baseline registry).
4) Post-event reality: timeliness labels + revision history.

Anchor artifacts:

- Use cases: [`NCAIF_Use_Cases.md`](src/01_Projects/2025-11_DCCE-CRDB/output/NCAIF_Use_Cases.md)
- MVP v2: [`NCAIF — Workflow patterns + MVP v2.md`](src/01_Projects/2025-11_DCCE-CRDB/output/NCAIF%20%E2%80%94%20Workflow%20patterns%20%2B%20MVP%20v2.md)

### 4) A3 — Governance phasing plan (data quality framing) (10–20 mins)

**Key message**: “Governance is data quality rules that make publishing safe.”

- Present 3 rails: Internal / GDX / Open Data.
- Ask: feasibility concerns; missing gates.

### 5) Feedback capture + close (15–25 mins)

**Key message**: “We will update artifacts based on feedback; this session reduces rework.”

- Summarize key feedback themes.
- Confirm next step(s): update MVPs, NCAIF structure, and slide deck guide.

## Decision prompts (Phase 1 unblockers)

### D1 — NCAIF design principle (Phase 1)

- Thematic vs user-journey vs hybrid.

### D2 — Phase 1 flagship products (confirm shortlist)

- Confirm which MVPs/products we publicly promise in Phase 1.

### D3 — Tiering stance (Tier 1 vs Tier 2)

- What is prescriptive Tier 1 (default) vs advanced Tier 2 (gated)?

### D4 — Recommended baseline endorsement authority

- Who (role/title) can publish a “recommended baseline” entry?

### D5 — Publishing gate (minimum)

- limitations + uncertainty statement required?
- draft → review → publish workflow required?

### D6 — Canonical boundary + crosswalk ownership

- Which boundaries are canonical per flagship product? Who owns crosswalks?

### D7 — Post-event timeliness stance

- What is the minimum publishable timeliness label + revision policy?
