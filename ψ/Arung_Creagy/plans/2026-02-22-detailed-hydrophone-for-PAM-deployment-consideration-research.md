These are crucial factors 
* Height from seabed
* Surrounding reef topography including coral reef masses and walls, cliff, steepness, walls
* distance from life hotspots within the habitat. need to find a balance because acoustically dominant species will distort the recorded soundscape
* habitat size dictates that representativeness of the recorded soundscape from one location

what we have to figure out in the field before deployment

- the exact spot that is a good representativeness of the habitat
