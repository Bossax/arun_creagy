1. Include "putting-data-in-a-box"  activity to demonstrate the usefulness of CDM.

	2. Prioritization of use cases from polling? would that must be made be higher-up officials instead of random operational staff who got invited to the meeting? 
	3. For DCCE officials from communication department , they look for communication material which would be produced by them, but they need sign off from the technical department. Proposing standardized and approved knowledge artifact pool could be of great benefits
