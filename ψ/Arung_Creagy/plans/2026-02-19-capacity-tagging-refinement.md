# Plan: CRI Phase 2 capacity-tagging dictionary

## Background
This plan is derived from the `/forward` handoff for the CRI Phase 2 tagging session. The session focused on turning the critique of "capacity tagging" into a concrete, literature-backed dictionary and wiring it into the CRI Phase 2 methodology. The research plan in [`Plan - 19 Feb 2026 - CRI phase 2 basic research.md`](src/plans/Plan%20-%2019%20Feb%202026%20-%20CRI%20phase%202%20basic%20research.md:1) was upgraded with dual prompt sets (Google Deep Research vs Consensus.ai), management-science framing, inclusion/exclusion criteria, extraction schema, and hypotheses. A v1 tagging dictionary was created in [`CRI_Capacity_Tagging_Dictionary.md`](src/01_Projects/2025-11_DCCE-CRI/output/CRI_Capacity_Tagging_Dictionary.md:45) and referenced from the tagging protocol section of [`CRI Phase 2 Methodology.md`](src/01_Projects/2025-11_DCCE-CRI/output/CRI%20Phase%202%20Methodology.md:87).

## Pending Tasks
- [ ] Extend the tagging dictionary with sector-specific indicators (e.g., water, health, housing, transport) grounded in additional case studies.
- [ ] Increase citation granularity for indicators where political scrutiny is expected (e.g., page/figure-level or short quote snippets).
- [ ] Align all CRI Phase 2 documentation to reference the tagging dictionary and avoid conceptual drift.

## Next Goals
- [ ] Choose one focus track for the next session: sector extension, propagation across CRI artifacts, or pilot tagging on real LPA/DOPA indicators.
- [ ] For the chosen track, define a concrete mini-plan (which files to touch, number of indicators or cases to process).
- [ ] Feed any new rules, exceptions, or sector-specific nuances discovered during implementation back into [`CRI_Capacity_Tagging_Dictionary.md`](src/01_Projects/2025-11_DCCE-CRI/output/CRI_Capacity_Tagging_Dictionary.md:45) as a v1.x update.
